This design brief offered an open subject matter, but asked that we select an area of interest that be rich in content. The subject matter chosen was a selection of Shakespeare Sonnets, packaged within a simple white packaged book.

The typeface combination between Scala Sans and Electra appeals to Shakespeare's historical roots, while bringing the content into a contemporary setting. The vector illustrations were created in response to each sonnet, responding to a contemporary environment to interest a wider demographic.

Illustrations were kept to a simple colour scheme reflecting contemporary illustration trends.
