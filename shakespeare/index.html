A contemporary response to William Shakespeare's famous sonnets. This particular brief offered an open subject matter, but asked that we select an area of interest that was rich in content. The solution presents thirty Shakespeare sonnets, chosen at random and packaged within a simple folded book.

The typeface combination between Scala Sans and Electra appeals to Shakespeare's historical roots, while bringing the content into a contemporary setting. Vector illustrations were created in response to each sonnet, while only appealing to contemporary situations.
