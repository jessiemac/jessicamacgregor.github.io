As an illustrator for the Monash Lots' Wife Magazine, it is important to translate an a article into an exciting visual piece. These vector illustrations have been created using my initial method of cut paper, then made into a vector illustration.

The first illustration for 2017 created was in response to Drug Depenalisation, an article curated by the editor of Lots' Wife. Using the juxtaposition between rehabilitation and the modern day 'black death' my response reflected a reluctance to help its victim, but rather banish the problem.

The second illustration for 2017 was in response to Classical Music. Reflecting upon the ideas of music, notes and a flood of emotional connection, I believed the illustration should reflect the flowing nature of musical notes. The Maestro and piano appear to be floating, losing themselves to the music they are both conducting and playing.
